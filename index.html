About SESA

SESA is the Software Engineering Students Association for students and alumni of the Software Engineering programme at the University of Auckland. We organise and run events for members such as career workshops, game nights, and industry talks with our sponsors.

Where necessary we also act as a liaison between the department and the SOFTENG students, raising any relevant issues (course content, difficulties in learning).

How to Join

To join SESA, fill out this form. Joining is 100% free. Follow the instructions on the form to become a member, even if you did this last year. Membership doesn't roll over so you need to fill out this form each year!

Once you have filled out the form, don't forget to join the Facebook Group, where most of our discussion happens!
